VADA PAV

The Speciality of Mumbai

Vada Pav, the quintessential street food of Mumbai, is a spicy potato fritter sandwiched in a bun, served with chutneys and fried green chilies. This humble yet flavorsome snack is fondly known as Mumbai's burger!
  • Is this an iconic Mumbai street food?Yes, it’s a staple in Mumbai!
  • Is it spicy?Yes, the chutneys and fried chili give it a punch!
  • Is this dish vegetarian?Yes, it’s entirely plant-based!

Overview of Ingredients in Vada Pav

  • Potatoes: The main filling of Vada Pav is made of spiced mashed potatoes shaped into patties.

  • Besan (Gram Flour): The potato filling is dipped in a gram flour batter and deep-fried to golden perfection.

  • Pav (Buns): Soft and pillowy buns that house the spicy potato vada. Lightly toasted with butter for extra flavor.

  • Green Chutney: A tangy and spicy chutney made from cilantro, mint, and green chilies.

  • Garlic Chutney: A fiery garlic chutney made with garlic, red chili powder, and coconut.


  1. Kadai (Deep Frying Pan): Essential for deep-frying the vada until crisp and golden.

  2. Spatula: To mix the potato filling with spices and sauté the mixture until well-blended.

  3. Butter Knife: To spread butter on the pav and toast it lightly before assembling.
