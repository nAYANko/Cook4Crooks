CHILAQUILES

The Speciality of New Mexico

A flavorful and traditional Mexican dish, chilaquiles are tortilla chips bathed in salsa and topped with a variety of delicious toppings like fried eggs, avocado, and cheese. Perfect for breakfast or brunch!
  • Is this an authentic New Mexican dish?Yes, deeply rooted in Mexican culture!
  • Is this recipe complicated?No, it's surprisingly easy!
  • Perfect for a quick, savory meal?Absolutely!

Overview of Ingredients in Chilaquiles

  • Tortilla Chips: Use store-bought chips or fry your own for the best flavor. They provide the crunchy base for the dish.

  • Salsa: The key ingredient! You can use either red or green salsa, depending on your preference. Salsa verde is a popular choice for chilaquiles.

  • Fried Eggs: A perfect topping for chilaquiles. The yolk adds richness to the dish.

  • Cheese: Crumbled cotija or shredded cheddar adds a salty and savory flavor.

  • Avocado: Creamy avocado slices to top off the dish. Fresh, delicious, and healthy.
